ABOUT US

STUDY Monitor® was launched in early 2015 with a mission to provide safety & security to school-going students. Parents concerned about their children's safety want to ensure that they reach school & back home safely. Busy, working parents need time to check their kids attendance & syllabus completion regularly, keep track of current home work & assignments, see how their kid is faring in school etc. A monthly parent-teacher meeting is not sufficient to meet this demand. With this concern in mind, the experts at STUDY Monitor® came up with an innovative mobile solution that could meet this demand and provide other valuable services to parents, students, teachers and the educational institution in general.


STUDY Monitor® is a real time mobile app that can be downloaded by students, teachers and parents. It is also available as a fully integrated online web application that can be used anytime and anywhere. Parents can keep track of their children's whereabouts just by tapping a button. Our integrated RFID & CCTV monitoring system track students in real-time, whether in the school bus or in the classroom. It assists in attendance tracking giving parents and teachers peace of mind.
