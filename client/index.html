THE 'C' s of STUDY MONITOR

COLLABORATE

This advanced module believes in generating tangible services for all people involved in an effective and easy way. Bridging the gap into one single stream, parents and teachers can have a one on one personalized conversation about students’ performance and progress. That apart, few other features are attendance log, reports, curriculum or syllabus schedules, assignment submission, school calendar or events can be conveyed day to day providing a stress-free access for both parent and teacher/ school admin.
COMMUTE

STUDY Monitor takes comprehensive measures to ensure student security and safety by monitoring and tracking school vehicles real-time. Parents can have an anxiety free day keeping track of their children by knowing details of bus routes and their current location via GPS mapping. The track detection rooted for school vehicles also gives details about vehicle speed providing alerts in case of emergency or immediate aid. The other features include automatic attendance via SMART ID cards , live CCTV monitoring on buses.

COMPETE

A systematic coordination of books in a school’s library has always been a tussle in any traditional school management system. Here our product inspires students to learn and collaborate well with educational resources be it in a classroom or a library. It allows to maintain a centralized access of book keeping and enriched study material that help librarians, teachers and students locate and share resources effectively. The automated tool keeps track of books that are due and also sends reminders when required.
